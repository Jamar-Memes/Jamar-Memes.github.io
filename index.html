Questions About Chrisland

What does Chrisland have to offer?

Chrisland offers more freedom than anywhere else, low taxes, affordable housing, healthcare that will not bankrupt you, great social services, and amazing educational opportunities to migrants.

 What is there to do?

Aside from living the best life there is, most people enjoy attending one of the numerous churches on Sunday, eating at a local cafe after work, and swimming in the clear, blue waters during the Summer. Further, Chrisland has world-class parks, stadiums, and theaters. These places host Christian, Country, and Rock music year-around, and a wide variety of sports and dramas.

What things should I know before visiting?

Key things to know before visiting or moving to Chrisland:
* Chrisland is a largely Christian, with Pentecostals (Protestants) accounting for the vast majority of Christians.
* Chrislanders are socially and economically libertarian-conservative, yet very giving and tolerant of others. Please return the favor.
*  While generous and robust social services are in place, these are to be used as a crutch and not a way of life. Abuse of them is punishable by labor and/or imprisonment.
* The death penality, while rarely used, is present in the Chrisland justice system. And     there is only one method of execution: hanging by the neck until dead in public.
* Like the United States, the Imperial System of measurement is used in Chrisland.
* All public schools teach from a Biblical worldview.
* Open carrying of firearms and weapons is legal for citizens and those with work visas.
* Wifi is provided for free throughout Chrisland thanks to the tax payers. Private businesses also have wifi available.
* Public and most government buildings/property are open to tourists, and tours are provided year-around. Video recording and pictures are allowed.
