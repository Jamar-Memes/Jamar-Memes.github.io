What Chrisland has to Offer

Chrisland offers top tier employment and educational opportunities to all.

More Jobs than We can Handle

Employment for citizens and foreigners is easy to come by. As a citizen, you will have access to high paying careers. With some only requiring a highschool education and the will to learn. Foreigners are not left out either. Chrisland's manufacturing and construction secters are always in need of workers. And best of all, healthcare and housing are covered. And those working over 35 hours are entitled to time-and-a-half pay! Most top tier companies even pay travel expenses. Work visas can be hard to obtain, but not here! Simply contact a Chrisland Representative, fill out the forms, and wait. Simple and easy.

Education

Education is free to all citizens and those employed! And college is affordable thanks to scholarships and government funding. Not to mention, many companies will foot your school bill provided that you work for them. Chrisland has one public college, Barnett University. Every year, 1,000 citizens and foreigners with work visas are randomly selected to attend there free of charge, with room and board covered..
