About Chrisland

Chrisland was founded on April 20th, 2023.

Location

Chrisland occupies the island of Cyprus, located in the beautiful Mediterranean Sea. The weather is amazing all year. And with lush forests, mountains, and endless beaches, Chrisland is a true crowd pleaser.

People

Chrisland is primarely made up of Americans, Greeks, and Israelies. With Turks, Mexicans, and tourists making up the rest. Chrisland maintains close relations with its neighbors and like-minded countries. 

Capital City

The capital of Chrisland is Montgomery. It is the largest by area and population. It also hosts Chrisland's only international arport, which is Trump International.

Industries

Though only an island with limited resources, Chrisland manages to punch above its weight. Medical equipment, aerospace materials, petroleum refining, chemicals, and electronics are Chrisland's top manufacturing sectors. With IT support, scientific research, and hospitality making up the bulk of the service sector.
